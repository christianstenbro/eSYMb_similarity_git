Welcome!

In this game, two people will play together as a group.
Another player will be the SENDER and you will be the RECEIVER.
The overall goal of the game is that the RECEIVER correctly identifies the concepts that the SENDER is trying to communicate.

Throughout the game, the SENDER and you (the RECEIVER) will see the same set of 4 concepts represented by the following images:

{{ for concept in C.CONCEPT_OPTIONS }} {{ endfor }}

The SENDER will also see a set of 4 patterns (each pattern is composed of 6 black lines), but you will not see these patterns.
There are 8 rounds. In each round, the SENDER will try to communicate one of the concepts to you by choosing and drawing one of the patterns.
Your objective is to guess which concept the SENDER is trying to communicate.
After you have received each concept once (rounds 1-4), you will have the chance to receive them again (rounds 5-8).
