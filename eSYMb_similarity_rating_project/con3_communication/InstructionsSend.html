Welcome!

In this game, two people will play together as a group.
You will be the SENDER and another player will be the RECEIVER.
The overall goal of the game is that the RECEIVER correctly identifies the concepts that the SENDER is trying to communicate.

Throughout the game, you (the SENDER) and the RECEIVER will see the same set of 4 concepts represented by the following images:

{{ for concept in C.CONCEPT_OPTIONS }} {{ endfor }}

Only you (the SENDER) will also see a set of 4 patterns (each pattern is composed of 6 black lines).
There are 8 rounds. In each round, one of the concepts will be pre-selected. Your task is to communicate the selected concept to the RECEIVER using one of the patterns.
To do that, first choose which pattern you want to use to communicate the concept, and then draw it on the rock surface using the drawing tool.
Your drawing will then be sent to the RECEIVER, who will guess which concept you are trying to communicate.
After you have communicated each concept once (rounds 1-4), you will have the chance to communicate them again (rounds 5-8).

Your objective is to help the RECEIVER identify the concepts you are trying to communicate with your drawings.

Remember:

  • Your drawing must match the chosen pattern, otherwise it may be rejected, and you will need to redraw it.
  • To draw a line, click and drag your mouse.
  • To move a line, click on its center and drag it to the desired position.
  • To rotate a line, click on one of the ends and drag it to the desired position.
  • To delete a line, double-click on it.
