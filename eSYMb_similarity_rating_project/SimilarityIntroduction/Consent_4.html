Study procedure

In this experiment, you will be asked to examine pairs of simple line drawings and rate how similar you find them.

The study consists of approximately 400 trials and is organized into 3 blocks with breaks between the blocks.

At the start of the experiment, you will receive further instructions and do a practice round to familiarise yourself with the task and the rating scale.
