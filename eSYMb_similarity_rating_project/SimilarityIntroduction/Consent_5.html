Withdrawal of consent

Participation is voluntary and you may withdraw your consent at any time without stating a reason.

If you wish to withdraw consent before finishing the experiment, simply leave the experiment without completing.

If you wish to withdraw consent after finishing the experiment, you must contact the research group and provide your Prolific ID.

The responsible investigators are Riccardo Fusaroli (Jens Chr. Skous Vej 2, building 1485, 640; email: fusaroli@cc.au.dk) and Murillo Pagnotta (murillo@cc.au.dk). You may also write to the data protection officer at Aarhus University (dpo@au.dk).
