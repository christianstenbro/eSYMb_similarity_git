Study procedure, risks, payment

Study procedure: In this experiment, you will be asked to examine pairs of simple line drawings and rate how similar you find them. The experiment includes a number of short trials divided into 3 blocks, and it should take about 30 min to complete. There will be breaks between the blocks. At the start of the experiment, you will receive further instructions and do a practice round to familiarise yourself with the task and the rating scale.

Risks: There are no known disadvantages or risks involved in this study beyond those encountered in normal everyday life.

Compensation: Your expected earnings for participating in the study are GBP 5.
