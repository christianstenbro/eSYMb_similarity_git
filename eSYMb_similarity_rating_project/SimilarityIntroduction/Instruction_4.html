Practice round

Before we begin with the experiment, you will go through a practice round to familiarise yourself with the interface.

You will see a pair of drawings.
Examine the drawings for a few seconds and decide how similar you find them.

To indicate your response, click and drag the slider below the drawings.
The rating scale ranges from not very similar to very similar.
