Welcome!

In this game, four people will play together split in two groups.
In your group, another player will be the DRAWER and YOU will be the GUESSER.
In the other group there will also be one DRAWER and one GUESSER.
The overall goal of the game is that the GUESSERS correctly identify the drawings made by the DRAWER from their own group.

There are 8 rounds. In each round, the two DRAWERS will each make a drawing. Once they are finished, you (the GUESSER) will be shown the two drawings, and you will be asked to guess which drawing was made by the DRAWER from your group.
At the end of each round, you will learn if your guess was correct or wrong.

Your objective is to identify the drawings made by the DRAWER from your group.
